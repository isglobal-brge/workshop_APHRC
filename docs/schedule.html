Virtual Participation

For those joining virtually:

  • All sessions will be live-streamed via Zoom
  • Recorded sessions available within 24 hours
  • Dedicated online support channel
  • Virtual networking sessions scheduled
  • Same access to servers and materials
Time Zone Considerations
  • Primary schedule is in East Africa Time (EAT)
  • Key sessions will be recorded for asynchronous viewing
  • Support available across multiple time zones
