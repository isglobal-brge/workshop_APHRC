Workshop Locations and Dates

The DataSHIELD Without Borders workshop will be delivered at the following three institutions across Africa:

Dates Institution Location
August 18–22, 2025 African Population and Health Research Center (APHRC) Nairobi, Kenya
August 25–27, 2025 Douala General Hospital (DGH) Douala, Cameroon
August 29–31, 2025 Institut de Recherche en Santé, de Surveillance Epidémiologique et de Formation (IRESSEF) Dakar, Senegal
